    Latest preprint

    CORTEG: Foundation Models Enable Cross-Modality Representation Transfer from Scalp to Intracranial Brain Recordings

    L Yang, Q Sun, B Van Dyck, EC Merino, MM Van Hulle

    Can a large model pretrained on scalp EEG be adapted to intracranial (ECoG) recordings? CORTEG bridges the two with an electrode-aware spatial adapter and a dual-stream tokenizer for low-frequency and high-gamma activity, then calibrates to a held-out patient in minutes — matching or beating task-specific baselines on finger-trajectory and audio-envelope regression.
